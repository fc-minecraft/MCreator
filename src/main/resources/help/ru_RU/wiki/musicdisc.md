**NOTE: Starting with MCreator 2024.4, music disc functionality is now part of the item mod element.**

This tutorial will guide you in the creation of a custom music disc.

## Creating a Music Disc

* Click on "Add New Mod Element"
* Choose "Music Disc" and name it.
* Set the properties of the music disc
* Add procedure events
* Save your mod element

### Music Disc properties

**Disc Texture:** It will be the texture of your music disc.

**Name in GUI:** It's the item name into the inventory.

**Music Disc description:** It's the description of the music disc (For example, C418 - cat).

**Creative inventory tab:** The inventory tab that your item will be inside.Which inventory tab your music disc will be.

**Has glowing effect?:** It will add a glowing on your music disc.Add a glowing on your music disc**.**
